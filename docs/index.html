SW Latinxs in Tech

Startup Weekend latinxs in tech is happening the weekend of Sept 29, 2017

About

The second largest ethnic group in America, the Latinx community is made up of 52 million people. So we're making this startup weekend the biggest in history. 5 cities. 1 Weekend. Pa' Lante!

Register now:

Oakland | Chicago | NYC | Miami | LA

Our vision for this event is to inspire potential entrepreneurs in the Latinx community to breathe life into their ideas and learn how to bridge the gap from ideation to business plan. Our main focus is on tech solutions, but we welcome all ideas that will help to create a future fueled by Latinx leadership and experiences.

In just 54 hours, you will experience the highs, lows, fun, and pressure that make up life at a startup. As you learn how to create a real company, you'll meet the very best mentors, investors, co-founders, and sponsors who are ready to help you get started.

We welcome everyone, regardless of your professional background. We foster safe spaces for full expression of yourself.

When one of us succeeds, we all succeed. Si Se Puede.
